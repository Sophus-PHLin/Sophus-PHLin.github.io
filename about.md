## About Me

Hello, I'm Peihan Lin(林沛晗), a Grade 1 student at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn), [School of Physics](https://www.phy.pku.edu.cn). My webname `Sophus` comes from the Norwegian mathematician, Marius Sophus Lie. My avatar is the Siberian Forest Cat.

I'm currently interested in optics and condensed matter physics, especially these topics:
- Non-hermitian optics and topological photonics.
- quantum optics, cavity QED and quantum dots.
- laser physics.
- topological condensed matter physics.
- strong-correlated condensed matter physics.
- link between CMT and optics.

My other interests include :

- linguistics and the relationship between languages.
- music.
