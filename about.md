## About Me

Hello, I'm Peihan Lin(林沛晗), a Grade 1 student at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn), [School of Physics](https://www.phy.pku.edu.cn). 

My webname `Sophus` comes from the Norwegian mathematician, Marius Sophus Lie. My avatar is the Siberian Forest Cat.

I'm currently interested in optics and condensed matter physics, especially these topics:
- Non-hermitian optics and topological photonics.
- Photonic chips.
- quantum optics, cavity QED and quantum dots.
- laser generated by condensed matter systems.
- topological condensed matter physics.
- strong-correlated condensed matter physics.
- Quantum many-body theory.

My other interests include :
- mathematics.
- scientific programming languages, such as Mathematica, Matlab.
- linguistics, especially the relationship between languages.
- music.
